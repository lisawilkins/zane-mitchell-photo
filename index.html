The Photographer

Zane Mitchell

Working the arena rail from first light to last ride, Zane Mitchell brings an insider's eye to rodeo and western sport photography. His work documents the full arc of competition — the tension at the chutes, the explosion of movement, and the quiet moments between.

Pursuing PRCA credentials and a career in professional sports photography spanning the NFL, NBA, MLB, and NHL, Zane brings the same discipline to his craft that every competitor brings to their event.
